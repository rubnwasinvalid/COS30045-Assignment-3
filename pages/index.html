Population Health at a Glance

This site provides two interactive views that help explore how Australia’s health outcomes vary across age groups and change over time. Use the visualisations below to inspect values, compare patterns, and identify differences between population groups.

How is Australia changing over time? Track life expectancy trends year-by-year to observe shifts and disruptions.
Which conditions are most prevalent? Compare long-term condition groups across age brackets to reveal concentration.
What differences appear by age? Identify which conditions rise sharply in older cohorts and which are consistent.

Long-term Conditions by Age (ABS Heatmap)

A matrix heatmap comparing condition groups across age groups. Colour intensity represents the proportion for each condition–age pairing, with hover tooltips for exact values and a sort control to surface the most prevalent conditions.
